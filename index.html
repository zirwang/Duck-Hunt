Welcome to Hunting Ducks (No, it's not duck hunt even though all the imagery is identical). Point and click to shoot the ducks before they fly away. Each level gives you less time to hit both ducks. BUT! Be careful, if you run out of shots it's also - GAMEOVER!

Click the screen to play as well as progress to the next level once both ducks have been shot.
